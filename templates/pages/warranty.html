Return & Warranty Policy

Effective Date: 2026

1. Overview

Trans Care Agencies is committed to delivering high-quality products. This policy outlines our return and warranty terms in compliance with Kenyan consumer protection standards.

2. Returns Eligibility

  • Product is defective or damaged upon delivery
  • Wrong item delivered

All return requests must be made within 7 days of delivery.

3. Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom-made or installed equipment
  • Used or altered products
  • Products damaged due to misuse

4. Return Process

  1. Contact us with proof of purchase
  2. Provide photos/evidence of defect
  3. Wait for approval before returning

5. Refunds

  • Approved returns may be refunded or replaced
  • Refunds via original payment method
  • Processing time: 7–14 business days

6. Warranty Coverage

  • Manufacturing defects
  • Structural faults under normal use

Warranty Period:
Institutional stoves: 12 months
Incinerators: 12–24 months

7. Warranty Exclusions

  • Misuse or negligence
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Unauthorized repairs/modifications

8. Repairs and Maintenance

We may offer repair services at a cost for damages not covered under warranty.

9. Limitation of Liability

Liability is limited to repair, replacement, or refund only.
